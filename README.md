# scout

  <img src="./assets/readme/hero.jpg" width="100%" alt="Scout: structured OSINT research — lawful people, company, and organization research with full provenance">

Scout makes research provenance a first-class requirement. Every claim traces to a source with URL, retrieval timestamp, and direct quote. Collection is bounded to the stated goal, and sources escalate from public web to paid databases only after explicit permission. Confirmed entities flow to Chronicle.

**Capabilities:**
- Tiered source waterfall with permission gates
- Full provenance: URL, timestamp, direct quote per claim
- Emits confirmed entities to Chronicle
